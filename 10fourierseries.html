Standing waves

In Section ___ we found the "standing waves" for a given 1D box, but those aren't the only possible waves that can exist inside the box. Linear combinations of these are possible as well: you can have multiple frequencies in the box at the same time. This is what happens on a guitar string. When you pluck a guitar string, you create waves of all frequencies, only the resonant frequencies remain, and the resulting sound is the linear superposition of all of them.
